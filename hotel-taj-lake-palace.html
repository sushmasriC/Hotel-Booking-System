About the Hotel

The Taj Lake Palace is a floating vision of white marble and mosaic, built in 1746 as the pleasure palace of Maharana Jagat Singh II. Today, it is one of the world's most romantic hotels, offering guests a unique blend of history, luxury, and hospitality. Enjoy boat rides, spa treatments, and royal feasts during your stay.
