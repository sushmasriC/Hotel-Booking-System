Explore India: Things to Do & Trip Recommendations

Goa

  • Relax on Baga and Anjuna beaches
  • Party at beach clubs
  • Visit Fort Aguada and Chapora Fort
  • Try Goan seafood and water sports
Recommended Plan:
  • Day 1: North Goa beaches, water sports, night market
  • Day 2: Old Goa churches, spice plantation, Panaji river cruise
  • Day 3: South Goa beaches, Cabo de Rama fort, sunset at Palolem

Kashmir

  • Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Visit Mughal Gardens
  • Gulmarg for skiing and cable car
  • Shop for pashmina and saffron
Recommended Plan:
  • Day 1: Srinagar sightseeing, Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens
  • Day 2: Gulmarg cable car, snow activities
  • Day 3: Pahalgam valley, river rafting, local shopping

Delhi

  • Explore Red Fort and Qutub Minar
  • Visit India Gate and Lotus Temple
  • Shop at Chandni Chowk
  • Enjoy street food and heritage walks
Recommended Plan:
  • Day 1: Old Delhi heritage walk, Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk food tour
  • Day 2: Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, India Gate, Connaught Place
  • Day 3: Akshardham, Dilli Haat, shopping

Hyderabad

  • Visit Charminar and Golconda Fort
  • Try Hyderabadi biryani
  • Explore Ramoji Film City
  • Shop for pearls and bangles
Recommended Plan:
  • Day 1: Charminar, Laad Bazaar, Chowmahalla Palace
  • Day 2: Golconda Fort, Qutb Shahi Tombs, Hussain Sagar Lake
  • Day 3: Ramoji Film City, Shilparamam crafts village

Mumbai

  • See Gateway of India and Marine Drive
  • Visit Elephanta Caves
  • Enjoy nightlife and street food
  • Shop at Colaba Causeway
Recommended Plan:
  • Day 1: Gateway of India, Colaba, Marine Drive, Chowpatty
  • Day 2: Elephanta Caves, Prince of Wales Museum, shopping
  • Day 3: Bandra, Juhu Beach, Bollywood tour

Bangalore

  • Visit Lalbagh and Cubbon Park
  • Explore Nandi Hills
  • Enjoy craft breweries and cafes
  • Shop at Commercial Street
Recommended Plan:
  • Day 1: Lalbagh, Cubbon Park, MG Road cafes
  • Day 2: Nandi Hills sunrise, Bangalore Palace, shopping
  • Day 3: ISKCON Temple, breweries, Indiranagar nightlife
